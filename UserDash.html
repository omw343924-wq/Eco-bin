Eco Tips

Recycle Smarter ♻️

Always remove batteries, chargers, and accessories before disposing of electronic devices. This improves recycling efficiency and keeps hazardous materials out of landfills.

  • Separate batteries from devices.
  • Keep e-waste clean and dry.
  • Donate working electronics whenever possible.
  • Use authorized EcoBin recycling centers.

Eco Tips

Extend Device Life 🌱

Before replacing your electronics, consider repairing, upgrading, or donating them. Extending the life of devices helps reduce e-waste and conserves valuable natural resources.

  • Repair devices instead of replacing them.
  • Donate working gadgets to schools or NGOs.
  • Delete personal data before recycling.
  • Choose rechargeable batteries whenever possible.
